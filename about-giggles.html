About Giggles

Fun Facts

A perfect day for Giggles means playing, having meals and napping, almost always consecutively. When he is displaying his superb timing and pouncing ability chasing toys and performing acrobatic flips, Giggles loves to stya near people and observe

Giggles loves to be given attention and is happy to follow you around and cuddle. Typically, he communicates by making small meows or chirms
